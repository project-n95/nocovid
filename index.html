NOCOVID United We Stand. Together We Win. National Organizing Coalition On Virus Information Distribution

COVID-19 doesn’t take sides.
Neither will we.

We have convened a non-partisan coalition of state and local government leaders, storytellers and entertainers, doctors and pandemic experts, health communicators and data analysts, historians and influencers. This group will communicate to all Americans – where they live, through people they trust, and in words that speak to them – the most current health, economic, and social facts and techniques to defeat the virus.
