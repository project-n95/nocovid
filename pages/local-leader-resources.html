Local Leader Resources

Local leaders know their own communities best. They are in a position to get things done. They can rapidly get out the information their community needs. And right now, the need is to encourage physical distancing to stop the coronavirus from spreading.

So we have created a Local Leaders Toolkit filled with links to resources they can use immediately, such as: best practices and resources from towns and counties across the country; how to tailor messaging to enhance physical distancing to their community; how to find local social media influencers to help spread their messages; how to make local supermarkets safer, and more.
