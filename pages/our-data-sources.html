Our Data Sources

The below data sources were used to identify evolving hotspot counties around the country that are most vulnerable to the effects of a sustained COVID-19 outbreak. In particular, we used these data sources to analyze at the county level COVID-19 cases per capita, case growth rate, social vulnerability, and local hospital capacity.
